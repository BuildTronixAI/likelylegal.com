About LikelyLegal?

The Idea

You've probably wondered: "Is it legal to [weird thing]?" And you couldn't find a straight answer because lawyers are expensive and Google is useless.

LikelyLegal? answers those questions. Daily. Short, confident, slightly funny. We're not lawyers, but we do our homework.

Audience

Contractors, small business owners, entrepreneurs. People who build things, run operations, and don't have a legal team on speed dial.

If you've ever wondered about employment law, tax deductions, or Florida's weird recording rules—this is for you.

Disclaimer
